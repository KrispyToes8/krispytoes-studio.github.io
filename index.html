Current Project

LastCallOperator

A UI-only dispatcher horror game where you take calls, choose ASK / DISPATCH / END, manage PRESENCE, and survive the shift—until the signal breaks.

  • 7–10 minute runs
  • 40 calls per shift
  • Multiple endings
  • No gore. No jump scares. Just creeping wrongness.
